# NP-Hard Fleet Logistics & Vehicle Distribution

## The Real-World Problem
Car distributors and transit operators in Ecuador face high operational costs and route inefficiencies when delivering newly imported or assembled vehicles to regional dealerships across the 221 cantons:
* **Logistics Constraints**: Car-carrier trucks have strict capacity limits (payload and volume).
* **Geographical Obstacles**: Cantons have varying topography (Sierra vs. Costa), varying delivery windows, and traffic conditions.
* **Cost Factor**: Inefficient routing increases fuel consumption, carrier wear, delivery times, and inventory holding costs.
