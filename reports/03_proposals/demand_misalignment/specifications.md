# Problem 2: Geographic Demand Disparity & Supply Chain Misalignment

## Appropriate Learning Algorithms
* **LSTM / GRU Recurrent Neural Networks**: Captures monthly seasonality and temporal sequence dependencies.
* **Prophet / SARIMAX**: Statistical time-series forecasting for localized regions.

## Computational Complexity
Modeling fine-grained time-series (hundreds of cantons and brands) is computationally intensive and suffers from high sparsity in small cantons.
